Creative Space in La Vila


Location : Olot, Girona, Spain
Date: Summer 2019
With: Mauro Turin and Group G5


This project was done as a group project with 8 other attendees from all around the world at the RCR Arquitectes Workshop on Landscape and Architecture in Girona Spain. The objective was to design a retreat space to promote creative activity for the users of the nearby RCR Laba Nave. This was done while trying to understand RCRâ€™s humanistic approach to architecture.
This project worked to answer the following design question: How can the existing Nave be transformed into an intimate retreat and a creative space integrated to its surroundings in a way to promote a more balanced and harmonious relation between man and nature?
