generative faces
javascript library p5.js || spring 2018

in the process of creating a generative landscape i generated this design. i wanted to create a 'landscape' of facial body parts somewhat inspired by childrens play books. each of the facial parts - lips, eyes, and nose - can come together in different positions on the canvas to create new faces. through clicks iterations can be created for the types of faces creating three new canvases with multiple permutations and combinations. in turn creating multiple generative faces.

click on the canvas to change the faces being generated
