Abstract Clock

p5.js Animation | Spring 2018


A 24 hour clock that acts as an abstract visualisation of the real thing. The larger pink circles reprsent the 60 seconds, the yellow circles represent the 60 minutes in an hour, and the blue ellipses, the 24 hours in a day.
